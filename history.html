Timeline History

Gaming Hotspot

Febuarary 13 2018

Created Cool Gamer TV Channel

Tyson with my freind Kaiss created the Cool Gamer TV YouTube channel at the start of 2018 during the school holidays in replacement for a previous YouTube channel that was involved with drama with a discord community and me being young at the time didnt know how to escape a drama situation.

Febuaray 27 2018

First Gaming Video Published

Cool Gamer TV was always designed to be a gaming channel so two videos later I uploaded my first gaming video which was a fortnite video and it was my first time playing fortnite, I played it on my xbox with me streaming the video back to my pc and recording it and with me not knowing anything about the game at the time made me look very silly.

March 2 2018

First Live Stream

When I started Cool Gamer TV I also wanted live streams so the content on Cool Gamer TV wasent just videos so on March the 2nd I did a very bad live stream that was intended for testing only however was really bad and cringe however it is still on my channel today.

April 15 2018

First Logo And Banner Change

My original logo and banner was made by Kaiss which helped me start the Cool Gamer TV YouTube channel however about two months later I wanted something that I made and was able to edit and something that looked at least halfway decent, so at the start of April I looked up a tutorial on YouTube and created the Cool Gamer TV - Gaming Matters slogan which was in my banner and my logo was a C in a certian font. This was one of the best logos and banners I have ever made.

September 23 2018

First Ever Vlog

In September I had some freinds over for the weekend at our local beach and we were all bored just sitting inside talking than because it was a nice night we decided to make the first ever vlog on Cool Gamer TV, even though it was decent and a bit cringe at times it is a popular video with almost 100 views.

October 6 2019

First Name Change

Near the end of 2019 I deicided it was time to change the name of Cool Gamer TV, all though minor it would set in place a lot of what the channel is today which is diversity and the ability to upload whatever I want, when I want. The new name was Cool Gamer TV - Tyson, with the - Tyson part meaning it was Cool Gamer TV (Gaming) and the - Tyson meaning my life and vlogs.

December 15 2019

First Fortnite Montage Published

Near the end of 2019 I decided to come back to YouTube after having a long break because of school and to be honest just not being bothered and I decided since I loved fortnite this was a easy way to play fortnite and make videos because all I had to do was make sure nivida shadowplay was working, edit the video like cut out the non kills and add some music and text and a video is done.

December 17 2019

First Daily Streak Of Videos

Near the end of 2019 I decided to do a daily video streak which was from December 17th Till December 25th where I would upload a video every day until the end of December 25th because I would be going on a family holiday around South Australia and straight after that I would be going to school which means I would need a lot of content to keep my audience entertained and I know uploading every day for a small channel isnt good for your stats but I needed to in order to insure that I would still get views for the long period of time where I was unable to upload.

March 4 2020

Second Name Change

In early 2020 when we moved into our new house I decided it was time for a more simple and clean name for Cool Gamer TV - Tyson and I couldnt think of anything so I decided to go to my viewers on instagram and YouTube and I had a bunch of suggestions then I compiled into my top 3 favourite and got my audience to vote in a poll and Gaming Hotspot was the winner so that was the new name for Gaming Hotspot.

June 1 2020

New Milestone

Gaming Hotspot is now at 270 subscribers which is close to 300 subscribers and my goal is to get to 300 subscribers by the end of 2020, I hope this goal is reachable because as soon as you reach a major milestone, YouTube recommends you much more than before.
